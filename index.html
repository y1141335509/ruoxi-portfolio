About me

My name is Katharine, but I also go by Ruoxi (若羲), originally from Jiangsu, China. My MBTI keeps changing, so despite the test results, I consider myself a socially introverted person. Obtaining an Aerospace Engineering degree was an accident. Let me try to frame my story differently: I found it more cost-effective to study something I couldn't teach myself, especially since I couldn't afford a wind tunnel. No, that story is too practical; let me try again. I pursued it because I've been a Formula 1 fan since my teenage years, and I wanted to build Kimi Räikkönen a stable and fast car. Much better. Having worked in various roles and companies, both long-term and short-term, large and small, I find startups the most appealing. Changing the world has always been my dream. Although I know it's a tall order, it's worth the attempt. Discussing business is engaging, just not in an intro. In my personal life, I've interacted with over 50 people in what might loosely be called
'dates'
, though not in the conventional sense. Through these experiences, I've aimed not just to change the world but also to save it, whenever I have the time.
